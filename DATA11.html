BIO

Aditi Gupta runs Menstrupedia, the world’s most innovative company when it comes to teaching and learning about periods. She has educated more than 50,000 girls about periods, trained 10,000 educators, and impacted the lives of 13 million girls worldwide. The Menstrupedia Comic is available in 20 languages and is used in 23 countries around the world. Her teaching techniques are culturally sensitive and widely accepted by over 1.5 lakh Indian Parents!
